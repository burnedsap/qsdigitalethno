Web AR

Ask the respondent to print out the marker on a standard A4 Sheet, black and white prints only, and place it in their environment where they would like to place the air purifier. Then, ask the respondent to open the link below on their phone (you can send it via Whatsapp), grant permissions to the camera, and ask them point it at the marker.

Make sure they are in a well lit room, with the phone camera having a good view of the marker. Ensure that there are no objects covering the marker, even partially. Then ask the respondent to take screenshots and share it with you.
